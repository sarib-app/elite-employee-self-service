Steps of Using Dashboard
  1. Access the Dashboard
    After logging in, you'll be taken to the Employee Portal Dashboard. This is your main hub where you can see all available services and your request statistics.
    Go to Dashboard
  2. View Your Statistics
    At the top of the dashboard, you'll see cards showing your Total Requests, Completed, Pending, and In Progress requests. Click on any card to filter and view those specific requests.
  3. Navigate Services
    The dashboard is organized into sections like 'Payment Requests', 'General Requests', and 'Attendance Files'. Each section has cards that link to different pages where you can submit requests, view lists, or check status.
  4. Access Your Profile
    Click on your profile icon or name in the top right corner to view your profile page. Here you can see your employee information, request counts, vacation balance, and loan details.
    View My Profile
Steps of How to Add Payment Request
  1. Navigate to Submit Request Form
    From the dashboard, click on 'Submit Payment Request' card in the Payment Requests section. This will take you to the request submission form.
    Go to Submit Request
  2. Select Expense Type
    Choose the appropriate expense type from the dropdown menu (e.g., Operational Expense, Employee Advance, etc.). Your employee information will be automatically filled in.
  3. Fill in Request Details
    Enter the request date, select your project, and fill in all required fields. The form has multiple steps - use the 'Next' and 'Back' buttons to navigate between sections.
  4. Add Items/Employees/Advances
    Depending on your expense type, you may need to add items, employee details, or advance information. Click 'Add Row' to add multiple entries. Make sure all required fields are filled.
  5. Upload Attachments
    Attachments are mandatory. Click 'Choose File' to upload supporting documents like receipts, invoices, or screenshots. You can upload multiple files.
  6. Review and Submit
    Review all the information you've entered. Once everything is correct, click the 'Submit Request' button. You'll receive a confirmation message with your request ID.
  7. Track Your Request
    After submission, you can view your request in the 'My Requests' page. Click on 'View' to see the full details and current status of your request.
    View My Requests
Steps of General Requests
  1. Access General Request Form
    From the dashboard, click on 'Submit General Request' in the General Requests section. This form is used for requesting documents like salary slips, employment letters, certificates, etc.
    Go to General Request Form
  2. Select Request Type
    Choose the type of document you need from the dropdown menu. Options include Salary Slip, Embassy Form, Employment Letter, Experience Letter, and more.
  3. Provide Details
    Enter any additional details or special instructions in the 'Details' field. This helps the admin understand your specific requirements.
  4. Upload Supporting Documents
    If required, upload any supporting documents. Click 'Choose File' to select and upload files. You can upload multiple files if needed.
  5. Submit and Track
    Click 'Submit Request' to submit your general request. You can track its status in the 'All General Requests' page. The request will show as 'Fulfilled' once completed.
    View All General Requests
Steps of Attendance File Upload
  1. Access Attendance Upload Form
    From the dashboard, click on 'Upload Attendance File' in the Attendance Files section. This form allows you to upload your monthly attendance records.
    Go to Upload Form
  2. Select Month
    Choose the month for which you're uploading the attendance file. Select the month from the date picker. Your employee information will be automatically filled.
  3. Upload Attendance File
    Click 'Choose File' to select your attendance file. Make sure the file is in the correct format (Excel, PDF, or CSV). The file should contain your attendance records for the selected month.
  4. Submit File
    Review the information and click 'Submit Attendance File'. You'll receive a confirmation with the record ID. The file will be processed by the HR department.
  5. View Uploaded Files
    You can view all your uploaded attendance files in the 'All Attendance Files' page. This helps you keep track of what you've submitted and when.
    View All Attendance Files
Frequently Asked Questions
How do I check the status of my request?
You can check the status of your requests in two ways:

1. My Requests Page: Go to 'My Requests' from the dashboard to see all your payment requests with their current status (Draft, Pending, Approved, Rejected).

2. General Requests List: Go to 'All General Requests' to see the status of your general service requests (Fulfilled or Pending).

Click on 'View' next to any request to see detailed information.
What file formats are accepted for attachments?
The system accepts common file formats including:

• PDF files (.pdf)
• Image files (.jpg, .jpeg, .png, .gif)
• Microsoft Office files (.doc, .docx, .xls, .xlsx)
• Text files (.txt)

For attendance files, Excel (.xlsx), PDF, and CSV formats are preferred.
Can I edit or cancel a submitted request?
Once a request is submitted, you cannot edit it directly from the portal. If you need to make changes or cancel a request, please contact the IT support team at itc@eliteresources.co with your request ID and the reason for the change.
How long does it take to process a request?
Processing times vary depending on the type of request:

• Payment Requests: Typically 3-5 business days after approval
• General Requests: Usually 2-3 business days for document requests
• Attendance Files: Processed within 1-2 business days

You can track the status of your requests in real-time through the portal.
What should I do if I forget my password?
If you forget your password, please contact the IT support team at itc@eliteresources.co. They will help you reset your password and regain access to your account.
Can I submit multiple requests at once?
Yes, you can submit multiple requests. However, each request must be submitted separately through the appropriate form. For payment requests, you can add multiple items, employees, or advances within a single request using the 'Add Row' feature.
